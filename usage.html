Usage

Description: This tool helps users analyze energy generation (Source) in the United States over Time.

Parameters The select boxes in the upper left will control how fossil fuels (coal, natural gas) and renewable energy (hydro, wind, solar) are displayed. They can be shown as individual plots, combined into one plot for their respective grouop, or suppressed.

The Scale select box will toggle the chart to show actual numbers or plot them on a log10 scale.

Individual lines can be toggled within the chart itself by clicking on their labels in the legend.
